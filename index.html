Analyzing U.S. Hospital Service Quality: Trends, Gaps, and Opportunities for Improvement
Personal Project

This analysis of U.S. hospital service ratings from 2015–2023 reveals a national stagnation—and even decline—in patient experience, especially in 2023. However, by focusing on high-impact areas like care transition, cleanliness, nurse communication, and staff responsiveness, hospitals have a clear opportunity to improve overall patient satisfaction.

Analyzing Cafe Rewards Offers with Looker Studio
Personal Project

I built a dashboard in Looker Studio to analyze customer behavior in a coffee rewards dataset, focusing on offer completions, transaction triggers, and demographic patterns. Key insights revealed that discount offers with high difficulty and long durations performed best, and that male users with undefined age earning \$50k–\$80k annually were the most likely to complete offers.
